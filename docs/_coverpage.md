# ros2mobile

> Stream your Android phone's sensors to ROS 2 — in one command.

- 10 sensors: IMU, GPS, Camera, Magnetometer, and more
- Zero-config Zenoh transport over WiFi or USB
- Live visualization with RViz 2 dashboard plugin
